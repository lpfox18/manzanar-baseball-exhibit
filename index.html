Welcome to this digital exhibit exploring the complex story of baseball during the Japanese American incarceration at Manzanar. Here, we delve into the multifaceted ways baseball was presented by the government, experienced by the internees, and remembered today. Through historical photographs, newspaper articles, and oral histories, we invite you to explore the varying narratives woven around America's pastime in the desert.
